Angela Davis

A Real Living hero

Angela Davis is an American born activist. She became a household name in the African American Community after she was falsely imprisoned in the 1960's by the United States Government.

Since a young age, Angela has continued to advocate for many disenfrancised communities. She has spread knowledge about varying political ideologies and been deeply involved in the Black Panther Party. She has written several books that are used in sociology courses around the country.
